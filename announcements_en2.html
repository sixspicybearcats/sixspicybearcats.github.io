Upcoming Events

Opening of Community Pool:

Date: July 22, 2017

Time: 12:00PM-2:00PM

Gowanus will be have its grand opening of the community pool this weekend. The community invites all residents to come take a dip!

Canal Clean-Up:

Date: August 20, 2017

Time: 9:00AM-3:00PM

The community is calling all the residents of Gowanus to gather together to help clean the canal. Both children and adults are encouraged to participate. Lunch and breakfast will be provided!

Outdoor Movie Night:

Date: August 17, 2017

Time: 6:30PM-8:30PM

A complimentary movie will be shown at near the Gowanus Canal this Friday night. Despicable Me 3 will be playing on the jumbo screen. All residents are encouraged to come watch!

Street Fair:

Date: August 23, 2017

Time: 11:00AM-6:00PM

On a sunny, bright day, kids come out along with their parents to have fun in the Gowanus Street Fair. There will be carnivals rides, cool slides, photo booth, face painting and more activities that anyone can join in!

Reported Incidents

Fallen Tree:

A branch fell off a tree due to last night’s heavy rainstorm. The branch was blocking a sidewalk, preventing people from safety walking to their destinations.

Car Crash:

This morning a car crash occurred near 9th Street and 3rd Avenue. Two were reported injured and were rushed to the New York Methodist Hospital.

Missing Manhole Cover:

Residents have reported of a missing manhole cover near 4th Avenue. The area has been closed off to prevent injuries.

Broken Fire Hydrant:

A broken fire hydrant was reported outside of Whole Foods Market. City Officials are currently working to fix this issue.

Current Programs

Free Tutoring for K-8:

Complimentary tutoring services are offered to students in K-8 in all subjects on Wednesday afternoons. Free snacks will be provided.

Swimming Lessons for all Ages:

Want to learn how to swim? Come take a beginner class at the recreation center and you’ll be jumping into the water in no time!

Tech Classes for Seniors:

Gowanus offers tech classes for senior citizens who want to learn more about using their computer, cell phone, etc. Services are offered every Tuesday at the senior center.

Art and Architecture Classes:

Enjoy painting and drawing? Come join us at the recreation center every Thursday to build new crafts!
